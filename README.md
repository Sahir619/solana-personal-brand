# solana-personal-brand

**An AI skill that builds a complete personal brand strategy on X for Solana founders — with their own voice, separate from their project.**

Most founders either copy their project's tone or post nothing at all. Both are missed opportunities. Your project account talks about the product. Your personal account should be about YOU — your thinking, your POV, your journey. This skill studies how the most followed Solana founders built their distinct presence and turns those patterns into a personalized strategy for any founder.

## Install

```bash
npx skills add sahir619/solana-personal-brand
```

## What It Does

Give it your founder profile and it produces:

- **Unique POV** — the one perspective you should own on X, specific enough that only you can credibly say it
- **Content pillars** — 3–4 recurring topics to build a coherent identity around
- **Writing style guide** — tone, sentence structure, jargon level, what to never do
- **Weekly posting rhythm** — realistic cadence that doesn't require posting every hour
- **Engagement playbook** — who to follow, how to reply to big accounts, how to join conversations
- **10 example posts** — written in your voice, ready to publish

## Inputs

```
1. Your name (real or pseudonym)
2. What you're building (1–2 sentences)
3. Background (technical / operator / researcher / etc.)
4. What you care about most (DeFi, AI, consumer crypto, etc.)
5. Natural communication style (casual / analytical / contrarian / etc.)
```

## Example Output

Sample posts from the Maya worked example (mobile-first DeFi wallet for emerging markets, non-technical product operator from Lagos/Nairobi):

**Origin story post:**
> *"I don't understand why it asks me so many questions."* She was trying to send $3 to her mother. Seven screens in. She gave up. That moment is why I'm building on Solana.

**Contrarian take:**
> "Un-banked" is the wrong frame for what we're solving. M-Pesa penetration in Kenya is 96%. These users aren't without financial infrastructure. They're without financial infrastructure that doesn't extract 8% per transaction. The problem is cost and custody, not access.

See the full 10-post output, all 7 sections, and the complete reasoning in [SKILL.md](SKILL.md#worked-example).

## What Makes This Different

Built on deep research of 5 distinct founder archetypes — the **Credible Eccentric** (Toly), **Internal Critic** (Mert), **Confessional Founder** (Frank), **Narrative Strategist** (Austin), and **Optimist Comedian** (Raj). The skill identifies which archetype(s) fit your background, then forces deliberate divergence — borrowing selectively from each but landing somewhere none of them occupy. The output cannot be a copy of any single archetype; if it reads as a Toly or Frank impression, the skill rewrites it.
